Must-Visit Places

Palakkad Fort

Historical fort built in the 18th century, perfect for history lovers.

Kalpathy

A picturesque village in Palakkad, famous for its ancient temple, chariot festival, and traditional agraharams.

Malampuzha Dam & Gardens

Beautiful dam with sprawling gardens, ropeway, and boating facilities.

Silent Valley National Park

Pristine rainforest with unique flora and fauna, ideal for trekking.

Nelliyampathy Hills

Scenic hills with tea and coffee plantations, perfect for nature walks.

Parambikulam Tiger Reserve

The reserve offers serene landscapes, forest safaris, and an immersive nature experience.

Kava - Malampuzha

A scenic viewpoint near Malampuzha offering breathtaking views of hills, reservoirs, and lush greenery, perfect for nature lovers and photography.

Varikkasery Mana

A magnificent heritage mansion in Palakkad, renowned for its traditional Kerala architecture and its role as a popular filming location for Malayalam cinema.
